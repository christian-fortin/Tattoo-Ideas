Magellan/History

Thoughts:

I love history and recently I read the book "Over the Edge of the World" which is about Ferdinand Magellan who is the first person to lead an armada around the world. He was Portuguese and in 1519 he left Seville with 5 ships towards the americas to find a passage to the Moluccas, Spice Islands. Magellan didn't make it back to Seville, and of the 260 men and 5 boats who started the Journey, only one boat and 18 men came back.


My friend has started their own tattoo studio now and they do this type of tattoo style. I saw it and didn't know what I wanted but I know I want something in this style. I want this tattoo to be a love child between the two above images. Dot tattoo work combined with history.

Locations:

  • Right thigh
  • Left thigh
  • back of left elbow
  • Back of right leg.

I grew up in a religious household. My father was the one who pushed religion to us. Push might not be the right word, more opened it to us.
